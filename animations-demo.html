How to Use

To change animations on your service cards:

  1. Open index.html
  2. Find the service card you want to change
  3. Replace the animation class (e.g., animate-blur) with your preferred option
  4. You can also adjust the stagger timing with stagger-1, stagger-2, etc.

Example:

<div class="service-card animate-scale stagger-1">
